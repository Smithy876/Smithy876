About

Jack of all trades:

Archaeologist, Designer, Anthropologist, Cartographer, Historian

  • Based in Madison, Wisconsin, on Ho-Chunk lands
  • Email: nsmith37@wisc.edu
  • In final year: BS Anthropology and History, minor in Archaeology from the University of Wisconsin–Madison

I've lived in Wisconsin all but one year of my life, which I spent as a baby in a near suburb of Chicago. I grew up in a small town called Holmen, about 20 minutes north of La Crosse, straddling the divide between rural and suburban. After high school, I was lucky enough to attend UW–Madison. Much like most people, my first year was rather rough—I though I was going to major in astrophysics, and boy was I ever wrong. I took an intro anthropology class on the advice of my mother, and from there it's been, well history.

After finishing my undergraduate degree, I'll be spending a year working in Madison, hopefully somewhere archaeology/cartography related, during which time I'll be deciding on grad school, becoming more active in my community, working toward some change, and hopefully getting some sleep.
